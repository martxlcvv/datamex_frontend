ABOUT US

Building Brighter Futures

Datamex College of Saint Adeline is committed to providing quality education that nurtures the mind, body, and spirit. Located in the heart of Valenzuela City, we have been serving the community since 2010.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality education that develops well-rounded individuals prepared for tomorrow's challenges.

Our Vision

To be a premier educational institution recognized for academic excellence, values formation, and community service.

Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Compassion, Innovation, and Service to the community.
